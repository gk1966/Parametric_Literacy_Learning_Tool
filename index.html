Learn Parameters

Brightness, contrast, gamma, levels, saturation, color balance, blur, sharpening, and effects are exposed as explicit values.

Inspect Image Data

Histograms, clipping warnings, metrics, before/after split view, and zoom inspection help users connect edits with visible outcomes.

Support AI Prompting

The tool translates parameter choices into structured prompts and optional AI Tutor feedback for image-editing literacy.

Developed by Georgios Korakakis, Assistant Professor, Department of Graphic Design and Visual Communication, University of West Attica (UniWA).
